Welcome to the Monty Hall Problem game!

This game is inspired by the Monty Hall problem brainer teaser. You are given a choice of three doors, with a winning prize lying behind one of the doors.

After making your initial selection, one of the losing doors will be elimiated and you are given the option of submitting with your original selection, or changing your selection to the other door.

The results table will keep track of your statistics as you play, including your success rate at finding the prize when maintaining your first selection as well as success rate when changing your selection to the other option before submitting.

If you play enough times, you will find out the your odds are greatly increased by change your selection after the losing door is eliminated (2/3 chance of winning in the latter situation vs. 1/3 chance of winning in the former).
